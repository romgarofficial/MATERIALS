Astro Boy

Who is Astro Boy?

Astro Boy, also known as Tetsuwan Atomu in Japan, is a beloved fictional character created by Osamu Tezuka in 1952. He is a powerful android with advanced capabilities, originally created to replace a scientist's deceased son. With his jet-powered boots, super strength, and a heart capable of experiencing human emotions, Astro Boy fights for justice and peace in a futuristic world filled with robots and humans alike. His adventures explore themes of technology, ethics, and what it means to be human.
